🛒 Halafood Sales, Product & Customer Insights
 ![Dashboard](dashboard.png)
📌 Overview
This project analyzes sales, customer behavior, and product performance for Halafood, an online Middle Eastern grocery store in Europe. The goal is to identify key business drivers and provide data-driven recommendations.

🎯 Business Problem
Halafood collects large amounts of data but lacks structured insights for decision-making in:
•	Inventory management
•	Customer retention
•	Product strategy
•	Market expansion

📊 Key Metrics
•	Total Orders
•	Total Sales (€134K+)
•	Active Customers
•	Average Order Value
•	Monthly & Daily Sales

📈 Key Insights
•	Revenue is concentrated in a small set of top products
•	Strong seasonal peaks (July, September, October)
•	Majority customers are one-time buyers
•	Austria & Italy are the strongest markets

🧠 Business Recommendations
•	Increase repeat customers using loyalty programs and targeted campaigns
•	Maintain stock availability for top-performing products
•	Align inventory with seasonal demand peaks
•	Expand partnerships with EU-based suppliers

🏗 Data Model
•	Star Schema (Fact + Dimension tables)
•	Optimized for Power BI performance
•	Supports scalable analytics and future AI

🔍 Strategic Analysis
•	PESTLE Analysis
•	SWOT Analysis
•	Market & operational insights

🛠 Tools & Technologies

•	Data Modeling
•	Excel Dataset

🚀 Project Value
